The Card component provides a flexible container for grouping related content and actions. Cards are commonly used for dashboards, product listings, and information displays.

## Basic Usage

Cards can contain various types of content including text, images, and interactive elements. They support different visual styles through props like `border`, `shadow`, and `radius`.

<Card
  padding="4"
  radius="l"
  shadow="m"
  direction="column"
  border="neutral-alpha-medium"
  horizontal="start"
>
  <SmartImage
    src="/images/cover.jpg"
    alt="Feature illustration"
    aspectRatio="16/9"
    radius="m"
  />
  <Column fillWidth paddingTop="20" paddingX="16" paddingBottom="16" gap="8" horizontal="start">
    <Text variant="heading-strong-m" onBackground="neutral-strong">Feature Title</Text>
    <Text variant="body-default-s" onBackground="neutral-weak">Description of this amazing feature and how it benefits users</Text>
  </Column>
</Card>

## Implementation Example

Here's how to implement a card with an image and text:

```tsx
<Card
  padding="4"
  radius="l"
  shadow="m"
  direction="column"
  border="neutral-alpha-medium"
  horizontal="start"
>
  <SmartImage
    src="/images/cover.jpg"
    alt="Feature illustration"
    aspectRatio="16/9"
    radius="m"
  />
  <Column fillWidth paddingTop="20" paddingX="16" paddingBottom="16" gap="8" horizontal="start">
    <Text variant="heading-strong-m" onBackground="neutral-strong">Feature Title</Text>
    <Text variant="body-default-s" onBackground="neutral-weak">Description of this amazing feature and how it benefits users</Text>
  </Column>
</Card>
```

Cards are highly customizable and can be adapted to fit various design requirements in your application.
